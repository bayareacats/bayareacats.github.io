Kittens

While they had a rough start in life, these kittens will live the rest of their lives in homes filled with love, pets, and play time.

Zizi

Zizi found a home in my neighborhood where she was rescued. She has a wonderful family and a cat sister named Kiki who enjoys licking her.

Maggie

Maggie is a special girl who is happiest being held and snuggled. She has a loving home where she is given the extra love and attention she craves!

Loki

Loki is a beautiful and loving boy who loves adventure. He is excited to continue being able to chew and snuggle with Rafa in his home in Los Altos.

Rafa

Rafa is a snuggle monkey who enjoys licking and sleeping on laps. He could not be happier spending the rest of his life with his brother Loki.

Cleo

Cleo was not rescued until she was 5-6 months old, and surprised us by being so friendly! She loves attention and lives happily nearby on Bubb Rd.
