Success stories

A few of our rescued kittens & TNR kitties

Cupertino • Rescues

Zizi

Zizi found a home in my neighborhood where she was rescued. She has a wonderful family and a cat sister named Kiki who enjoys licking her.

Maggie

Maggie is a special needs girl who is happiest being held and snuggled. She was taken in by the Orphan Kitten Project, and will be available soon.

Loki

Loki is a beautiful and loving boy who loves adventure. He is excited to continue being able to chew and snuggle with Rafa (see their Instagram).

Rafa

Rafa is a snuggle monkey who enjoys sleeping on laps. He could not be happier spending the rest of his life with his brother (see their Instagram).

Cleo

Cleo was not rescued until she was 5-6 months old, and surprised us by being so friendly! She loves attention and lives happily nearby.

Ash

Ash was taken in by Gatos de la Noche. He has the appetite of a horse and loves playing with his new brother Milo.

Karaage

Karaage was taken in by Gatos de la Noche and has a brother named Boo! He is a purring machine and can be found on Instagram.

Salem

Salem was taken in by Gatos de la Noche and now lives with a brother named Russel. She is an adventurous girl always looking to play!

Milk & Mocha

Milk & Mocha were fostered through The Dancing Cat! Mocha is a curious explorer and Milk is very relaxed and chill (their Instagram).

Bambi

Bambi was fostered through The Dancing Cat! Bambi enjoys burrowing in blankets and playing with his brother Bourbon (their Instagram).

Keanu
