Donations

Our neighborhood cats and kittens could use your help. If you would like to get involved in trap-neuter-return (TNR) or fostering cats or kittens, do not hesitate to reach out. Donations of trap bait (wet or dry cat food or tuna), puppy pee pads, or other cat supplies are all gladly accepted. You can also donate via PayPal. Monetary donations and supplies will be shared with the rescue organizations that take in the kittens. Contact me at bayareaTNR@gmail.com with any questions or if you want to get involved.
