Support our mission

To decrease the number of unfixed cats in the community. Bay Area Cats is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Our organization provides resources and enables the community to do trap-neuter-return (TNR). We spend time training, educating, rescuing kittens, and assisting with TNR efforts. We help connect people to spay/neuter resources and support those with financial need.

If you would like to get involved in trap-neuter-return (TNR) or fostering, please email info@bayareacats.org. Our community cats and kittens could use your help! You can also donate via PayPal or Venmo, and through our Chewy or Amazon Wishlist.

Join our Facebook group for support with TNR. If you have cats or kittens in your yard, follow the Getting Started steps.
