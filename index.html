Support our mission: to decrease the number of unfixed cats in the community

Bay Area Cats is a 501(c)(3) volunteer-run nonprofit focused on prevention. We provide spay/neuter, trap-neuter-return (TNR), and rescue support to reduce overpopulation.

Our programs

Fill out our contact form with the details of your specific situation. Here's how we can help:

  • Feral cats & kittens in Cupertino and Sunnyvale
  • Spaying female pet cats
  • Pregnant cats: pet, stray, or trapped pregnant cats in any city in the Bay Area
