Donations

Our neighborhood cats and kittens could use your help. If you would like to help trap-neuter-return (TNR) and rescue kittens in the Phar Lap neighborhood, donations of trap bait (wet or dry cat food, tuna, sardines), Royal Canin BabyCat wet and Royal Canin BabyCat dry for nursing mothers and kittens, or Rescue disinfectant to clean the traps are all gladly accepted. You can also donate via PayPal. Monetary donations will be shared with the rescue organizations that take in the kittens. Contact me at cupertinocats@gmail.com with any questions or if you want to get involved.
